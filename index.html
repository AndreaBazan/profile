PROJECTS

Navidad es Jesus (NEJ) is a solidarity campaign to promote the true meaning of Christmas in society through solidarity visits. Bring joy and hope to children in need, and foster a spirit of solidarity among our volunteers.
Every year, between the months of July and August, hundreds of young people gathered in different countries around the world participate in the MVC Missions, which promotes a spirit of solidarity among young people and involves them in the construction of a more human world. The missionaries' dynamic is, mainly, to provide spiritual and material help to the poorest communities in each country. At the same time the missionaries have a space to grow their faith in an integral way: faith in the mind, faith in the heart and faith in action.
